Обновления emmanuelbassil, Banditoz:

Reworked drug metabolism extensively. Affects alcohol, opioids, nicotine and another handful of drugs most visibly. More details on the wiki.
Reworked how protein/sugar maluses work; where it now depends on how much sugar/protein was actually absorbed and not how much is in your stomach. This means IV sugar/protein or protein on skin (for Skrell) now contributes to toxicity as well.
Added Naloxone, which cleans out opiate metabolites. 5u should remove 25u of opiate relatively quickly (2 per tick.) Made with ethanol, inaprovaline, and phoron catalyst.
Therapeutic doses of anti-depressants make you less prone to hallucinations. ODing does the opposite.
Therapeutic doses of methylphenidate makes you more prone to hallucinations.
ODing on methylphenidate now causes seizures. Can be reversed by soporifics.
Alcohol now provides a painkilling effect that scales up with dose.
Lowered deletrathol OD to 10; metabolized very slowly and lasts long.
ODing on nicotine can lead to heart damage. Nicotine metabolite products are counted towards OD; so chain smoking places you at higher risk.
Higher quality tobacco is less toxic and prone to side effects than lower quality tobacco.
Cigarettes now consistently deliver some nicotine for as long as they're lit; as opposed to giving it all at the beginning and emptying out.
Allergies for tramadol and oxycodone have been migrated to opiate; the metabolic product of both. Please double check your character's allergies.
Allergies for alcohols have been migrated to the parent ethanol alcohol type. If you were allergic to vodka, you're now allergic to all alcohols!
